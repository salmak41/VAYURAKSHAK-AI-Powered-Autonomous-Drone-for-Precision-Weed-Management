The Challenge of Modern Weeding

Labor Intensity & Cost

Manual weeding is incredibly time-consuming and expensive, often requiring 50-60 labor days per acre.

Inefficiency of Broadcast Spraying

Traditional tractor-mounted sprayers waste over 70% of herbicides, applying them indiscriminately on both crops and soil.

Environmental Damage

Chemical runoff pollutes soil and water tables, harms biodiversity, and leads to herbicide-resistant "superweeds."

Accessibility Issues

Large equipment cannot easily access small, irregular, or water-logged fields, which are common in Indian agriculture.

Our Innovative Solution

VAYURAKSHAK is an autonomous quadcopter drone that acts as a precise, flying weed-killing machine. Our system combines AI, GPS, and robotics to perform selective weed spraying while significantly reducing chemical use.

Core Function: See, Map, and Spray

See

Uses a high-resolution camera to scan the field and identify weeds with computer vision.

Map

AI identifies weeds and GPS tags their location for precise targeting and future analysis.

Spray

Triggers micro-sprayers to apply herbicide only on the weeds, reducing chemical use by 80-90%.

Technical Innovation

1

Edge-AI on Raspberry Pi

We run an optimized Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) directly on the Raspberry Pi using TensorFlow Lite, enabling real-time weed detection without any internet connection.

2

Custom Micro-Spraying Mechanism

Our proprietary nozzle and pump system delivers herbicide in precise micro-droplets only onto the leaves of identified weeds, minimizing drift and waste.

3

Automated Geotagging & Mapping

The system doesn't just spray; it creates a detailed GPS map of weed locations, providing farmers with valuable data for future decision-making.

4

End-to-End Automation

From autonomous takeoff and flight based on pre-mapped coordinates to real-time processing and spraying, the system requires minimal human intervention.

5

Cost-Effective Platform

By leveraging the Raspberry Pi ecosystem and efficient design, we keep the solution affordable and accessible for the average Indian farmer.
